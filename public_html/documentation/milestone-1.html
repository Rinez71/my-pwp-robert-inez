Milestone 1

Purpose

My PWP will be used as a personal portfolio for the projects that I create. I will include a small about me section which will serve as my virtual elevator pitch. I will also be displaying projects that i have completed

Audience

The audience of my PWP will primarily be potential employers, clients, and other developers. This will website will be my "call to action" when presented with an opportunity to get my name out there.

Goal

The goals I have for my website are as follows:

  • Attract freelance clients
  • Aquire a full or part-time internship or employment
  • Serve as a gallery to promote my projects

My PWP's Idealized User

Doyle Barrett

Technology

  • Devices: Primarily uses Dell Inspiron 5000 running Windows 10 and Samsung Note20 running Android 11
  • Proficiency: Proficient with technology. Doyle is very comfortable behind a keyboard and enjoys using his mobile outside of work
  • Passion for technology: Doyle enjoys the advantages technology grants him and tries his best to stay up-to-date with new technologies

Attitudes and Needs

  • Doyle is using my site to gain information about me as a person/employee, gauge my development skills by viewing gallery of projects, and contact me for a project
  • Doyle needs a website revamped for his insurance/brokerage firm
  • Doyle is using my site because it allows him to gain a plethora of information about me in a short amount of time at one place

Frustrations

  • Doyle finds it frustrating that he is not usually able to see developer's projects before speaking with them
  • Doyle is also frustrated that he cannot gauge what kind of person he is hoping to hire for a project

Role

  • Power User

User Story

As a power user, I would like to view credentials and personality of a potential hire.
